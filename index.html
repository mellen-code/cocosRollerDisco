Roller Disco for Everyone

Since our groovy inception in 2007, Coco's Stardust Roller Disco has been serving up rad roller disco parties every weekend, March through November. Each night's theme reflects the fabulous roller rink parties of the 70s, 80s and 90s. Come join our talented team of djs, dazzling performers, and friendly MCs for an unforgettable experience you'll want to come back to again and again!

Legwarmers, sequins, spandex, glitter, unitards, blue eyeshadow, visors = optional but heartily recommended!
